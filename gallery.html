Zen Garden Salad

This meticulously arranged salad features 12 varieties of microgreens, edible flowers from our rooftop garden, and a yuzu-miso dressing. The presentation draws inspiration from Japanese zen gardens, with each element placed precisely to create a harmonious composition.

  • Ingredients: Microgreens, edible flowers, yuzu, white miso
  • Techniques: Precision plating, color balancing
  • Pairing: Jasmine pearl tea
