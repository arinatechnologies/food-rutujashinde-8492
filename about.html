About TeaChai

Crafting authentic tea experiences since 2010

Our Story

2010

Founded in a Small Kitchen

Started as a passion project by our founder experimenting with tea blends in a home kitchen.

2013

First Retail Location

Opened our flagship store in downtown Portland, introducing our unique blends to the public.

2016

National Distribution

Partnered with organic grocery chains to bring TeaChai products nationwide.

2019

Sustainable Certification

Received Fair Trade and Organic certifications for all our tea products.

2022

Global Expansion

Launched international shipping to share our teas with customers worldwide.
